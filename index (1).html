About Us

Best Golden Inox

Welcome to Best Golden Inox!

We are a leader in engineering and mineral industries, and we are proud to offer the best metal solutions and engineering consulting to our customers worldwide. Since our founding, we strive to provide the best quality products and services that meet the diverse needs of our customers.

What distinguishes our company is our strong commitment to excellence and innovation, as we use the latest technology and equipment to design and manufacture high quality products with superior performance. Our team includes a group of qualified and experienced engineers and technicians who always strive for the highest quality and performance standards.

Our products include a wide range of metals and alloys, including stainless steel, aluminum, copper, nickel, titanium, and more. We offer a customized solution for each customer, as our products are designed according to their individual requirements and specific applications.

Services

Tuyauterie

Direct interlocutor from the design to the DESP file, we realize in steel, stainless steel or thermoplastics all types of networks containing fluids or gas, frozen or overheated, in high or low pressure.

Boilermaking

BGI manufactures small and medium capacity boiler assemblies in steel and stainless steel such as tanks, exchangers, boilers, technical frameworks, skids as well as mechanically welded assemblies.

Mechanical

Our engineers are able to meet all general mechanical requirements. Our design office integrates CAD and DAO software to carry out our customers' projects.

Maintenance

For one-off, flat-rate or direct intervention as well as maintenance contracts, part of our staff is placed full-time with our customers to carry out their maintenance work.
